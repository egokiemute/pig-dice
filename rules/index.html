How To Play

Pondice is a two-player dice game where players take turns rolling the dice to build their score.

1

🎲 Click Roll Dice to roll

On your turn, click the Roll Dice button to roll the die. The result is added to your current turn score.

2

➕ Continue rolling to increase your score

You can keep rolling as many times as you like, adding each roll to your current score. The bigger the risk, the bigger the potential reward.

3

📥 Click Hold to save your score

When you're happy with your current score, click Hold. Your current turn score is added to your total score and your turn passes to the other player.

4

🔁 Turn passes to the other player

After you hold, your opponent takes their turn. Players alternate until one reaches the winning score.

5

🏆 First to the winning score wins!

The first player to reach or exceed the selected winning score is declared the winner. Watch out — rolling a 1 loses your entire current turn score and passes the turn!

Winning Scores

Before starting a game, players choose a winning score. The first to reach it wins.
