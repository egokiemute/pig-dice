The Developer

The person behind Pondice.

Henry Ponda

Web Developer · Designer · IT Consultant

Henry Ponda is a web developer, designer, and IT consultant with a drive for creating meaningful digital experiences. He holds an Associate Degree in Computer Science and is currently studying Informatics at Varna Free University.

He is the founder and lead designer of Ferndril Design Co., where he helps businesses build strong brands and websites. Henry is also a member of YDSG, a local NGO in Varna focused on sustainability, climate action, and volunteering.

Pondice was created as part of his Web Technologies coursework and reflects his interest in combining technology, learning, and fun.
